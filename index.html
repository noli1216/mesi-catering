Welcome to Mesi Catering

Delicious Ethiopian food delivered to your home! Whether for events, family meals, or regular orders, our catering service is always ready. We accept orders for weddings, holidays, graduations, and birthday celebrations.

We Specialize in Ethiopian Cuisine

We prepare famous Ethiopian dishes like Injera, Doro Wat, Kitfo, and Tibs with care and love. Please let us know your preferences, and we will prepare your meal with great taste and passion.

About Us

We are a small business passionate about sharing the rich flavors of Ethiopian cuisine. We prepare fresh and clean traditional dishes like Injera, Doro Wat, Kitfo, Tibs, and special fasting foods with care and love.

Though small, we focus on quality and making every meal special for our customers. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we cook every dish with dedication and heart.
